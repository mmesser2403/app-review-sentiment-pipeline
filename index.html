About the Project

What We Are Building

Companies receive thousands of app reviews every week. Reading them manually is slow and inconsistent. Our system automates this — scoring, flagging, and surfacing trends so product managers can act fast.

📈

Sentiment Scoring

Predicts a 1–10 rating from free-text reviews with a confidence score alongside every prediction.

🚩

Smart Flagging

Automatically flags spam, single-word reviews, empty negatives, and low-confidence predictions before they affect results.

🕑

Trend Tracking

Tracks app scores over time so teams can see the impact of updates and monitor weekly complaint trends.

👥

User Dashboard

A lightweight dashboard for filtering, sorting, and exploring scored reviews — built for non-technical users.

Requirements

Key Requirements

What the system must do (functional) and how well it must do it (non-functional).

⚙ Functional Requirements

  • Score reviews on a 1–10 scale
  • Provide a confidence score (0–100%) per prediction
  • Filter and sort reviews by category and score
  • Flag spam, single-word, and empty negative reviews
  • Flag apps scoring below 4.0 for inspection
  • Flag apps scoring 10.0 for advertisement
  • Display weekly most-complained features
  • Track score trends before and after app updates

📋 Non-Functional Requirements

  • Minimum 80% prediction accuracy (target 85–90%)
  • Flag 95%+ of single-word and empty negative reviews
  • Detect spam with 95% precision and 90% recall
  • Support 300 concurrent users
  • Return results within 5 seconds under normal load
  • Process 10,000 reviews per week without failure
  • Confidence scores accurate to ±2%
  • Retain 6 months of trend history
Phase 3 Plan

Planned Iterations

Our Phase 3 construction work is split into two focused iterations.

Iteration 1

Fix the Baseline & Core Pipeline Improvements

  • Rescale model output from 0–1 to 1–10
  • Improve preprocessing and preserve sentiment signals
  • Add language detection
  • Implement flagging for single-word and empty negative reviews
  • Switch to stratified splitting and cross-validation
  • Explore alternative models with confidence score support
  • Set up experiment tracking
Iteration 2

Confidence Scoring, Flagging Logic & Dashboard

  • Implement confidence score generation
  • Build spam detection module
  • Add aggregate score threshold flagging
  • Build Streamlit dashboard with filtering, sorting, and flags
  • Set up persistent output storage with timestamps
  • Enable trend tracking and weekly complaint display
